Rich's Solitaire

How to Play Rich's Solitaire

Who is Rich??

Rich is someone I grew up with. He invented this game when we were kids. I give credit where credit is due.

Playing in Real Life

Shuffle a deck of cards. Deal cards from the top of the deck one at a time, turning each card face up as you deal. In addition, as you turn each card up, say the name of a card as described below. If the card you turn up matches the name you say, you lose. You win if you get through all 52 cards without a match.

Say face values of cards in order from Ace to King, without regard to suit. When you reach King, start over at Ace. That means you recite, "Ace, Two, Three, …, Queen, King, Ace, Two…."

Computer Play

When you click the Play button, the computer does all the work, randomly shuffling a deck of cards and displaying them one at a time till the game ends because you win or lose.

Winning happens surprisingly infrequently. It's fun to see how many plays are needed to win!
